About Us

Dona@balos chef School and hospitality is a private owned culinary School situated in Maseru Lesotho. It was founded by Chef Donald Moletsane in 2013, and is currently the only school of its kind in the mountain kingdom. Dona@balos is legally recognized with the government of Lesotho, and hence it is a trusted skills development organization. Now Dona@balos is providing a program for students who wish to become vibrant professionals
